# Interview Day Reminders

1. **For technical questions:**
   - Start with the "why" before the "how"
   - Use concrete examples from your experience
   - Acknowledge trade-offs
   - Connect to banking/regulatory context

2. **For behavioral questions:**
   - Use STAR format
   - Be specific with metrics and outcomes
   - Show learning and growth
   - Connect to VP-level responsibilities

3. **For design questions:**
   - Clarify requirements first
   - Draw diagrams (ASCII is fine)
   - Discuss trade-offs explicitly
   - Consider scale, reliability, governance

4. **General tips:**
   - It's okay to think aloud
   - Ask clarifying questions
   - Admit what you don't know
   - Show enthusiasm and curiosity
